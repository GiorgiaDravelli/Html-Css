My Story

I was born and raised in the suburbs of Parma, as a child I dreamed of becoming an archaeologist, journalist, veterinarian and doctor.

Instead, I ended up studying foreign languages and literature and subsequently earned a degree in audiology.

During the 5 years of professional experience in audiology studies I have carried out studies in another sector that I am passionate about.
In fact, I obtained two masters, becoming a dog trainer and veterinary assistant.

In 2022 I felt the need for a change, so I decided to quit my job and go on a trip around Australia.
After a few months of travel and reflections I decided it was time to learn how to do something new.

For this reason I started a master's degree in Full Stack Development thanks to Start2Impact University.

Traveling made me realize that I love novelties and the possibility of shaping things to my liking, which is why I decided to enter the world of digital development.

Giorgia Dravelli

Full Stack Developer
